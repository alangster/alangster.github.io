A Quick Class on Ruby Classes


This week’s DBC prep has been focused on Ruby classes. A simple way to think of classes in Ruby is that a class is a group to which objects can be added. All of the objects of a class share certain attributes as well as methods that may be called on them. The values of the aforementioned attributes, however, may vary among the objects.
